why you should visit our restaurant?

the idea of ​​our Syrian kitchen is to derive its diversity from the Syrian nature,regions and cities. Every Syrian city and region has something that distinguishes it from the dishes between Damascus, Aleppo, Homs,Hama and the cities of the Syrian coast and others.this is what made Syrian cuisine so rich with its many delicious dishes.The idea of ​​the restaurant is to collect these dishes in one place that includes the most delicious recipes and prices that suit you. our restaurant is characterized by its comfortable seat and beautiful view,and we also have a home delivery service.our first and last goal is the convenience of the visitor,and you are welcome.
